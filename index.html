My GitHub Gaming Career

My journey in software development is defined by a deep-rooted passion for browser-based architecture. Rather than relying on massive game engines, I specialize in building custom, high-performance web frameworks from the ground up using core JavaScript, CSS, and HTML5. By hosting directly on GitHub, I ensure that my projects are not only accessible globally but also serve as open-source blueprints for others who want to learn how web games are structured.

Our History

UntitledC🎂keStudios was born from the realization that modern gaming was becoming too bloated. We started our history in a small local workspace, experimenting with how minimal code could produce maximum interaction. We quickly realized that the GitHub ecosystem was the perfect laboratory for our work—it allowed us to push updates instantly, track our iteration process, and invite the community to peek under the hood of our development cycles.

Over the years, we have transitioned from creating basic logic puzzles to building sophisticated interactive engines. We have survived numerous framework shifts and browser updates, always keeping our core mission intact: creating games that are fast, fun, and entirely browser-native.

Privacy Policy

At UntitledC🎂keStudios, our privacy philosophy is simple: we cannot lose your data if we never collect it. We are proud to maintain a strict "Zero Data" policy.

We do not utilize cookies, we do not require user logins, and we do not employ any third-party tracking software. When you navigate to our games, you remain completely anonymous. Your gameplay experience is yours alone, and we intend to keep it that way.
